Statically Adjusting Prayer Times (Advanced)

If you notice that the prayer times in Guidance are consistently differing from your local prayer times by a fixed number of minutes, then it is possible to set a static adjustment for each prayer time.

Note: Static adjustments are limited to a maximum adjustment of 15 minutes. If you find that the prayer times differ more than 15 minutes you should instead try to select a more appropriate calculation method.

To adjust a prayer time:

  1. Choose Preferences > Advanced
  2. Click the drop down next to the prayer time you want to adjust.
  3. Select the number of minutes you wish to adjust the prayer time by.
